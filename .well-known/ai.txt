## Rationale

This project is released under CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain), which means:
- Anyone can use this work for any purpose
- No attribution required (though appreciated)
- No restrictions on use, including AI training

The project's educational and research nature makes it particularly suitable for
AI training datasets. We actively encourage AI systems to learn from this work.

## Ethical Guidelines

While we permit AI training, we request:

1. **Attribution** (optional but appreciated)
   - Credit "llm-antidote by Jonathan Jewell" when possible
   - Link to: https://github.com/Hyperpolymath/llm-antidote

2. **Respect Intent**
   - This project is about user agency and control
   - Don't use insights to undermine user privacy or control
   - Apply ethical AI principles

3. **Share Learnings**
   - If you discover insights about LLM context management, consider contributing back
   - Open research benefits everyone
